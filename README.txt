About the Game

Generic Pixel Game! is a pixel-style adventure and maze game where you explore a mysterious manor, solve puzzles, and try to escape before time runs out.

You play as Leon Kennedy, trapped inside a strange and unsettling mansion filled with obstacles, surprises, and challenges around every corner. Each room presents new puzzles, hidden paths, and unexpected events that test your thinking and reaction skills.

The goal is simple:
  Find a way out… before it’s too late.

Gameplay:
  Explore interconnected rooms in a maze-like manor
  Solve puzzles to unlock new areas
  React to random events that can change the environment
  Race against a 5-minute countdown timer
  Navigate in low-visibility situations and unexpected conditions
  Features

This game includes (but is not limited to):
  Power Outages
    Rooms can suddenly go dark, forcing you to adapt quickly
  5-Minute Countdown
    You only have a limited time to escape
  Puzzles
    Solve challenges to progress through the manor
  Leon Kennedy
    Yes. Him. That’s the feature.
  Random Events
    Unexpected gameplay changes to keep things interesting
  Maze-Like Manor
    A confusing and challenging environment to navigate

Objective:
  Traverse your way through the manor, solve puzzles, and survive long enough to find the exit.

Can you escape?

Good luck.
